Proposer Information

1. Find A Suitable Space

You can browse all available spaces or use the Space Finder to bring up spaces that are suitable for the kind of display you want to put on and are available when you need them.

2. See Space Information

& Proposal Steps

After finding suitable spaces, check their information page to learn more about setting up displays and the steps needed to make a proposal.

3. Contact Staff

After checking all the information for your chosen space, learning from past archives, and filling out any needed forms, contact the relevant staff mentioned in the space’s page.
